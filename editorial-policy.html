Editorial policy

The principles used to select topics, assess information, review recommendations and correct published material.

Editorial purpose

Ventoro Style publishes practical editorial content for men across Style, Grooming, Health & Fitness, Gear & Tech, Money & Career and Life.

Articles should help readers understand a topic, compare realistic options or make a more informed decision. Content should not rely on humiliation, fear, artificial urgency or narrow ideas about how men should look or behave.

Research and source selection

Writers should use sources appropriate to the topic. Primary sources, official documentation, recognised professional organisations, academic research and direct product specifications should be preferred when available.

Claims should be presented with the appropriate level of certainty. A limited study, personal observation or early trend should not be described as an established fact.

Product and service recommendations

Recommendations should be based on clear criteria. Depending on the subject, these may include:

  • Build quality and durability
  • Practical usefulness
  • Fit, comfort or usability
  • Price relative to comparable alternatives
  • Repairability, support or expected lifespan
  • Meaningful limitations

Higher price does not automatically mean higher editorial ranking. Products should not be recommended only because they are new, fashionable or positioned as premium.

Health and fitness content

Medical, mental health, nutrition and exercise topics require careful language. Articles should distinguish general education from individual medical advice.

Health claims should be supported by credible medical or scientific sources. Writers should avoid diagnosing readers, promising specific outcomes or presenting a single routine as suitable for everyone.

Money and career content

Financial and career articles should explain principles, choices and risks. They should not offer personalised investment instructions, guaranteed income claims or unrealistic career outcomes.

Tax, employment and financial rules differ by country and may change. Readers should be told when professional or jurisdiction-specific advice may be necessary.

Corrections and updates

Material errors should be corrected when identified. An article may also be updated when important facts, prices, regulations, product specifications or accepted guidance change.

Minor spelling, formatting and clarity edits may be made without a correction note. Material changes that alter the meaning or conclusion should be handled transparently where appropriate.

Commercial influence

Editorial conclusions should not be purchased or determined by a manufacturer, retailer, service provider or advertiser.

If Ventoro Style later introduces sponsorships, advertising, affiliate links or supplied review products, the relevant commercial relationship should be disclosed clearly near the affected content.
